About Us



HOW WE OPERATE


Techno BlacQ Innovations (PTY) LTD. is a 100% Black owned solution-based company that places much of its business methodology on innovation and this is achieved through the adoption of new custom-made technological tools and resources that are designed to enhance human daily activities. Innovation through technology is a key concept that has helped to advance humanity and over the last decade there has been a rise in demand for companies that come up with high tech solutions that will meet the needs and demands of a fast-paced business world that is evolving and moving towards the 4th Industrial Revolution which requires new approaches in conducting business and satisfying customer needs according to the set standards that customers are now accustomed to.
The idea behind the formation of Techno BlacQ Innovation is to supply businesses and individuals with high end innovative tech solutions and tools that will be tailored to enhance the efficiency and productivity in the way they conduct their daily activities whether it is to fulfill a need or to supply excellent services to clients.


VISION
Our vision is to enhance humanity with proper and efficient technological models and principles that will increase their productivity, convenience and efficiency in their daily activities through the creation of custom-made software’s and hardware’s that are designed to bring modern solutions to meet modern needs.


OBJECTIVES
To be the first company in South Africa to help in advancing technology to all types of businesses and help them be more innovative in how they adopt new strategies and tools that will ensure and increase in their overall productivity and business profitability.


APPROACH
The company has designed an integrated approach that will involve the fusing of technology, psychology and physiological principles and apply them to solve modern problems and seeks to bring growth and stability to the conduct and operations of businesses.
